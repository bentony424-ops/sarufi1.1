Who We Are

A circular economy, engineered for real communities.

At Sarufi, we "imagine tomorrow" and work with communities to promote the tenets of a circular economy — through waste recycling and expanding access to clean, affordable, reliable and sustainable energy, by scaling innovative technological and financial concepts alongside industry best practice.

Sarufi is professionally run, complete with a board of governance and expertise spanning decades across the globe. We work directly with our host communities and the underprivileged to drive socioeconomic empowerment, environmental awareness, and the eradication of poverty through employment creation.
