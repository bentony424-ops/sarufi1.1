Who We Are

About Us

At Sarufi, we "imagine tomorrow" and work with communities to promote the tenets of a circular economy — through waste recycling, and by expanding access to clean, affordable, reliable and sustainable energy. We do this by scaling innovative technological and financial concepts alongside industry best practice.

Sarufi is professionally run, complete with a board of governance, backed by expertise spanning over 40 years across the globe through our shareholders and partner network.

We work with our immediate communities, and the underprivileged in society, to ensure their socioeconomic empowerment, environmental sensitisation, and the eradication of poverty through employment creation.

Environmental Policy

Reducing our footprint, by design.

Sarufi is continuously working to improve environmental impact through the recycling of PET waste bottles, the deployment of energy-efficient management solutions, cost-saving capacity building programs, and forward-thinking technological products.

  • → Ensuring our activities are safe for our employees
  • → Complying with, and exceeding, local legal frameworks and global best practice
  • → Preventing pollution from processes used across our business
  • → Minimising waste produced in all parts of our business
  • → Integrating environmental considerations into future business decisions

Sarufi is also proud to be an equal-opportunity employer, committed to a diverse, respectful and inclusive workplace for every member of our team.
