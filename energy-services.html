Energy Management Services

Energy that pays for itself — and the planet.

Sarufi helps organisations become more energy-efficient, sustainable and resilient — through audits, financing, technology and capacity building that turn energy costs into energy savings.

Our Approach

Empowering organisations to use less, and use smarter.

We deliver innovative solutions and comprehensive capacity-building programs to help you optimise energy consumption, reduce greenhouse gas emissions, and foster a culture of environmental stewardship.

01 / Assessment

Energy Audit & Assessment

We conduct comprehensive energy audits to identify areas of energy waste and inefficiency, performing detailed assessments to develop customised recommendations for improvement.

  • Site-wide energy waste & inefficiency identification
  • Customised improvement recommendations
  • Cost-benefit analyses for informed decision-making
02 / Financing

Innovative Energy Projects Financing

Clean energy shouldn't be blocked by capital constraints. We structure flexible financing models to fit your organisation's needs.

  • Lease-to-own (PPA)
  • Energy-as-a-service
  • Outright purchases
  • Strategic partnerships with PE firms specialising in clean/renewable energy
03 / Systems

Energy Management Systems

We design and implement robust systems to monitor and control energy usage in real time.

  • Advanced metering & monitoring installation
  • Real-time energy consumption data
  • Dashboards & reporting tools for optimisation
04 / Efficiency

Energy Efficiency Solutions

We design and implement energy-efficient technologies tailored to your specific industry needs.

  • Energy-efficient lighting, HVAC & equipment upgrades
  • Renewable solutions — solar panels & wind turbines
  • Offset strategies for existing energy consumption
05 / Capacity Building

Training & Capacity Building

We conduct workshops and training programs to educate employees on energy conservation practices.

  • Customised organisational capacity-building programs
  • Guidance on regulatory compliance & energy policy
  • Support toward sustainability certifications
06 / Monitoring

Performance, Monitoring & Reporting

We track energy performance, analyse data, and provide regular progress reports that keep improvement measurable.

  • Trend identification & benchmarking
  • Ongoing improvement recommendations
  • Support for LEED or ENERGY STAR certification
07 / Consulting

Sustainability Consulting

We offer strategic guidance on sustainability initiatives, helping you set goals that are ambitious and achievable.

  • Sustainability action plans & carbon footprint reduction
  • Life-cycle assessments to identify environmental hotspots
  • Mitigation strategy development
